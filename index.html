"The Luxury of Happiness" is a play on words that captures the beautiful contradiction at the heart of human joy. Luxury suggests both lavishness and high cost, while happiness is often seen as both a fundamental right and something elusive and precious.

Happiness can be as free as a child's laughter or as costly as years of therapy. It can uplift us instantly or require years of work to achieve. This blog explores the many facets of happiness - when it comes easily, when it's hard-won, and everything in between.

About This Blog

Exploring the Contradiction

This blog exists in the space between happiness as a fundamental right and happiness as a precious luxury. It's about recognizing that joy can be both freely given and hard-won, both simple and complex, both guaranteed and elusive.

"Happiness is the luxury of being able to choose what brings you joy, and the privilege of having the resources to pursue it."
